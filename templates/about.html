About the Website

Many organizations still rely on manual or fragmented processes to report and handle maintenance issues, which often leads to delays, miscommunications, and unresolved requests. The proposed Maintenance Ticketing System provides an all-in-one platform for submitting, tracking, and managing tickets across departments such as IT, plumbing, electrical, and carpentry. It aims to make maintenance reporting faster, more organized, more efficient and easier for employees, maintenance personnel, and administrators.

About the Creator

Hello! I’m Cezar Dominic Teope, the creator of the Maintenance Ticketing System. I developed this project with the intention of improving how maintenance concerns are reported, tracked, and resolved within an organization. The idea came from observing how many workplaces still rely on manual reporting methods, such as verbal communication, handwritten logs, or scattered messages, which often lead to delays, lost reports, and miscommunication.

Inspired by real scenarios in schools and offices, as well as modern ticketing systems used in the IT industry, I wanted to create a simple and efficient solution that fits general maintenance operations. This system was built to provide a centralized and organized platform where employees can easily report issues and maintenance teams can manage tasks more effectively. My goal is to offer a tool that enhances transparency, reduces response time, and creates a reliable history of maintenance activities. Thank you for taking the time to learn about the project. I hope this system even though it's a simple project contributes to a smoother and more structured maintenance workflow.
